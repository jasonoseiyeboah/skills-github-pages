POWER BOY THE HERO PART 1


In a city there lived a teenager named Marvin. Marvin lived in Harrisville. One day his mother grounded him for breaking her vase and he got upset and ran away. Marvin went to his uncle's factory for a job and his uncle accepted him. He also called Marvin's mother. Then his mother said it is okay for Marvin to apply for the job and also stay with his uncle.

The next day Marvin woke up early because it was his first day of work. He went to work with his uncle that day. He worked till his shift was over and went for his break. The next hour it was time for his last shift of the day. While he was arranging the chemicals one of the chemicals got contact with his body. After that he went to the restroom to wash off the chemical. Then he went back to his uncle's house because his shift was over.

One morning just like any other morning, Marvin went to work. After finishing all his shifts for the day, there was a thunder storm so he waited for it to pass. While he was going home, he was strack by lighting. However, he was still okay. That night he then found out that he had super powers. The next day he decided to use his gift to help people. So he become a super hero and named himself Power Boy. He also quitted his job to get more time.

One day, the major of Harrisville visited power boy and gave him a liar, super weapons and also paid him to save the city. That day the crime computer in his lair gave him an alert that, the super villain, dark blade has escaped from his cell and committing crime. Later power boy got the information that Dark Blade was heading to the city bank. So power boy went to the bank. After a minute Dark Blade barged in to the bank and power boy swiftly locked all the doors and windows. Then threw lighting out of his eyes at Dark Blade. Then he took him to jail.
